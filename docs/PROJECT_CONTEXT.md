# Project context

## Title

Danúbia em:  O Desaparecimento


## Occasion

The game is a birthday experience created for Danubia.

Birthday date: 29 June 2026.

After completing the game, Danubia will receive a present from
her family.

## Experience

- Short 2D pixel-art adventure.
- Approximate duration: 10 to 15 minutes.
- Played on a computer mirrored to a television.
- Primary input: PlayStation controller.
- Keyboard is the fallback.
- Humorous and emotional tone.
- Simple enough for a non-regular gamer.
- Failure returns the player to the latest checkpoint.

## Story

On her birthday, Danubia discovers that her husband, son, daughter, three dogs,
and six cats have disappeared.

She explores the house and collects three clock fragments. The fragments open
a portal to Paris.

The family was taken by Monsieur Minuit, an eccentric Parisian clockmaker who
believes perfect moments should be frozen before they end.

The animals escaped during the temporal transport and became scattered across
Paris. Danubia rescues them in the order in which they joined the family:

1. Pudim
2. Zoe
3. Drogo
4. Pirata
5. Batata
6. Pituca
7. Brecko, Lelo, and Pure together

Danubia reaches Monsieur Minuit's workshop, restores the flow of time, and
rescues her family.

The conclusion communicates that meaningful moments do not need to be frozen.
They matter because life continues and creates new moments.

The physical cellphone is revealed only after the final in-game instruction:
"Olhe para sua família."
